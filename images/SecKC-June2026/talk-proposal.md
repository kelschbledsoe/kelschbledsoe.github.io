## Title of your talk
Computer Science Education Starts with Community

## Description of the Talk
Access to computer science education in high schools has expanded significantly, but access alone does not guarantee meaningful learning experiences or clear pathways into technology careers. After transitioning from my own technology career into teaching, I saw firsthand how disconnected students can be from real technical career pathways and how little support many educators have in building those opportunities. This session explores the current state of high school CS education, the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations, what I’ve learned rebuilding a CS department, and practical ways local professionals can help support teachers, inspire students, and strengthen the next generation of technology talent.

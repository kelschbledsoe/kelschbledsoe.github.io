Hello and welcome to my blog!

After a successful career in the technology industry, I made a career transition to teaching! I'm currently a high school computer science teacher based out of Kansas City, MO. My courseload includes introductory programming, advanced software development, cybersecurity, and computer hardware courses. I focus on project-based learning and creating experiential learning opportunities for my students. I'm also the director and head coach for my school's Esports program.

I have worked in many different companies, industries, roles, and programming languages, with each opportunity teaching me valuable lessons about myself and the tech industry. I hold an M.S. in Computer Science with a concentration in Computing Systems from Western Governors University, along with a B.S. in Computer Science & Engineering from Michigan State University.

My technical interests include secure software development, automation, and increasing the accessibility of technology and technology careers.

My passions outside of work include traveling, basketball, Michigan State University (Go Green!), and personal finance.
